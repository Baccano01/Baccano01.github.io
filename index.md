MSc in Computer Science Student, [Sapienza University of Rome](https://www.uniroma1.it/it/).

I am an MSc in Computer Science student at Sapienza University of Rome. I received my BSc in Computer Science at Sapienza University of Rome (30/30 cum laude) and was an Assistant Researcher at [Sapienza Prometheus Laboratory](https://prometheus.di.uniroma1.it/) which is one of the university's [Computer Science Department Research Labs](https://www.di.uniroma1.it/it/laboratori-di-ricerca).

Other current extracurricular roles:
- External Research Collaborator at Sapienza Prometheus Laboratory.
- Ground Control Software Engineer at [Sapienza Space Team (SST)](https://www.sasa-aerospace.it/space-team/).
- Member of [Sapienza Aerospace Student Association](https://www.sasa-aerospace.it/).

My main research interests lies on the fields of Artificial Intelligence (AI) & Systems, applied on interdisciplinary fields such as Distributed Deep Learning, Space, Outdoor/Indoor Positioning, High-Performance Computing, and Low-Latency Networking.

I build and optimize systems at the intersection of (non-exhaustive list):
- Advanced Machine Learning (Deep Learning & Computer Vision)
- Big Data Computing
- Distributed Deep Learning
- Concurrent Systems
- Advanced Computer Architectures
- Multicore and Parallel Programming
- Computer Networks Performance (Queuing Theory)
- Advanced Algorithms
